About PriceCheck

A simple, free tool built to help everyday shoppers make smarter choices at the supermarket.

Why We Built This

Standing in a supermarket aisle trying to decide between a 600g pack at $3.90 and a 1kg pack at $5.80 — while someone's waiting for you at the checkout — is not a fun maths problem. And most of us get it wrong.

Supermarkets design their shelf layouts to make this comparison difficult. Products are placed at eye level for a reason, pricing is inconsistent, and promotional labels are designed to trigger impulse decisions rather than rational ones.

PriceCheck was built to fix that. It's a free, no-signup, no-ads-in-the-way tool that does one thing well: tells you which product is cheapest per unit, in under 30 seconds.

What We Stand For

  • Free to use, forever. No paywalls, no premium tier, no login required.
  • No data collection. We don't store anything you enter into the calculator.
  • Ad-supported, not pay-to-win. Ads keep the lights on — they never influence the results.
  • Honest information. Our shopping tips are based on consumer research and common sense, not brand partnerships.
  • Built for Singapore shoppers first — but usable anywhere in the world.
